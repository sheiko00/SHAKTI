The Story

From India's ancient gardens
to Italy's craft ateliers

Shakti — the Sanskrit word for the primordial cosmic energy — represents the living force that flows through all of nature. It is from this ancient wisdom that our brand takes both its name and its soul.

Our botanicals are sourced with reverence from India's most storied growing regions. Each ingredient undergoes Italy's exacting cold-process formulation — preserving bioactive integrity from field to face.
